## Case Study Snapshots

## Levene Energy: Business Insights and Reporting Training
We delivered a Business Insights &amp; Reporting Training program designed specifically for Levene Energy’s operational and management teams. The training focused on developing the ability to transform operational data into actionable insight, improve reporting accuracy, and support decision-making across daily business activities. Participants learned practical techniques for analyzing key performance indicators, monitoring operational efficiency, and producing meaningful reports that drive strategic actions.
The program included hands-on exercises using real operational data, interactive workshops on reporting best practices, and post-training follow-up to ensure adoption of new methods. Within three months, participants demonstrated improved reporting accuracy, faster decision-making, and greater alignment between operational activity and strategic objectives, enhancing overall operational performance and reliability.


## Egbin Energy – CSR Initiative: Tech to Rural Program
Digital World Corporate partnered with Egbin Energy to implement Tech to Rural, a CSR initiative providing technology-focused training for youth in rural communities. The program covered ICT fundamentals, Graphics Design, Cybersecurity, Web Development, Digital Marketing, and Data Analysis, equipping participants with both foundational and practical digital skills.
The initiative combined classroom instruction, hands-on labs, and project-based learning to ensure participants could apply new skills in real-world contexts. We also provided mentorship, follow-up guidance, and a showcase platform where participants could demonstrate their projects, reinforcing confidence and employability.
Through this CSR initiative, young people gained market-relevant digital competencies, opening pathways for further education, freelance opportunities, and entry into the digital economy. The program strengthened Egbin Energy’s community engagement and positioned the company as a socially responsible leader supporting youth development and rural empowerment.
